About RETECH

Leading the way in sustainable electronic recycling since 2020, we're committed to creating a greener future through responsible e-waste management.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

At RETECH, our mission is to create a sustainable future by promoting responsible recycling of electronic devices. We strive to reduce electronic waste in landfills, recover valuable materials from old devices, and protect sensitive data through secure disposal methods.

  • Reduce e-waste in landfills
  • Recover valuable materials
  • Protect sensitive data

Founded in 2020 by a group of environmental engineers and tech enthusiasts passionate about making a difference.

Our Vision

We envision a world where electronic waste is minimized, and the valuable resources within our devices are continuously recirculated into the manufacturing process. Our goal is to make recycling electronics as easy as buying them.

Today, we've grown into a nationwide service helping individuals and businesses responsibly dispose of electronic devices.

Our Journey

2020

RETECH Founded

Started with a small team of 5 passionate environmental engineers and tech enthusiasts with a vision to make electronics recycling accessible.

First Recycling Center

Expanded our services nationwide and partnered with major electronics retailers to provide convenient drop-off locations.

2023

Green Initiative Launch

Launched our tree planting initiative - for every 100 devices recycled, we plant a tree to further our environmental commitment.

2024

Future Vision

Expanding digital platform with new features to make recycling even more convenient while reducing our own carbon footprint.

Our Recycling Process

1. Assessment

We evaluate your device and provide a fair quote based on its condition, age, and recyclable components.

2. Data Wiping

We securely erase all personal data from devices using industry-standard methods to protect your privacy.

3. Recycling

Devices are carefully disassembled and materials are sorted for proper recycling or environmentally safe disposal.

Why Recycle Electronics?

Electronic waste (e-waste) is one of the fastest-growing waste streams globally. When recycled properly:

  • Valuable materials like gold, silver, copper, and rare earth elements can be recovered
  • Toxic substances like lead, mercury, and cadmium are kept out of landfills
  • Less mining of new materials is needed, reducing environmental impact
  • Your privacy is protected through proper data destruction

At RETECH, we follow industry best practices for electronics recycling, including secure data wiping and responsible materials recovery.

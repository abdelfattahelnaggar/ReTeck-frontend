Why Recycle Kitchen Appliances?

Kitchen appliances contain valuable materials like metals, plastics, and electronic components that can be recycled. Proper recycling:

  • Reduces landfill waste
  • Conserves natural resources
  • Prevents harmful chemicals from entering the environment
  • Reduces energy consumption compared to manufacturing new products

At RETECH, we ensure all appliances are dismantled and recycled properly, with hazardous materials handled safely according to environmental regulations.

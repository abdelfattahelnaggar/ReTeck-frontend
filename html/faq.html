Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recycling services, payment options, and more.

General Recycling Process Payment & Pricing Shipping & Delivery Account & Security

Retech is an eco-friendly recycling platform dedicated to responsible electronic waste management. We provide services to recycle phones, laptops, kitchen appliances, and other electronics. Our mission is to reduce e-waste and promote sustainable technology use.

Retech contributes to environmental sustainability by recycling electronic waste responsibly, refurbishing devices when possible, and ensuring proper disposal of hazardous materials. By extending the lifecycle of electronics and recovering valuable materials, we reduce the need for new resource extraction and minimize landfill waste.

We accept a wide range of electronic devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, kitchen appliances, gaming consoles, televisions, audio equipment, and various small electronics. If you're unsure about a specific device, please contact our customer service for assistance.

Our recycling process is simple: First, submit details about your device through our online form. We'll provide an estimated value based on the information provided. Then, ship your device to us using our prepaid shipping label. Once we receive and verify your device's condition, we'll process payment according to your preference. Devices are then refurbished or recycled responsibly.

Before sending your device, please follow these steps: 1) Backup your data, 2) Perform a factory reset to remove personal information, 3) Remove any SIM cards or memory cards, 4) Disable any activation locks or Find My Device features, 5) Include the charger and accessories if possible. For detailed instructions specific to your device, please visit our device preparation guide.

We take data security very seriously. While we recommend performing a factory reset before sending your device, we also have a certified data wiping process that meets Department of Defense standards for all devices we receive. This ensures that all personal information is completely removed before recycling or refurbishing.

The value of your device is determined based on several factors including the make, model, age, condition, functionality, and current market demand. We use a comprehensive pricing algorithm that takes into account these factors to offer you a fair and competitive price for your device.

We offer several payment methods including direct bank transfer, PayPal, check by mail, or store credit (with a bonus value). You can select your preferred payment method during the quote acceptance process. Once your device is verified, payment is typically processed within 3-5 business days.

If our inspection determines that your device is in worse condition than described, we'll send you a revised offer based on its actual condition. You can choose to accept the new offer or have your device returned to you at no cost. We strive for transparency in our pricing process to ensure customer satisfaction.

After accepting our quote, you'll receive a prepaid shipping label via email. Simply package your device securely in a box with proper cushioning, attach the prepaid label, and drop it off at the specified carrier location. For larger items, we can arrange a pickup service. All shipping costs are covered by us.

Yes, all shipments are insured up to the quoted value of your device. In the rare event that a package is lost or damaged during transit, we'll work with the shipping carrier to file a claim and ensure you're compensated appropriately. We recommend taking photos of your device before shipping as additional documentation.

The entire process typically takes 7-14 days from the moment you ship your device to when you receive payment. This includes shipping time (2-5 days), processing and inspection (1-2 days), and payment processing (3-5 business days). You can track the status of your recycling request through your account dashboard at any time.

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. Fill in your details including name, email, and password. Verify your email address through the link we send you, and your account will be ready to use. With an account, you can track multiple recycling requests and manage your payments more efficiently.

We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Our privacy policy outlines how your data is collected, used, and protected. We never sell your personal information to third parties. All payment processing is done through secure, PCI-compliant systems to ensure the safety of your financial information.

Yes, you can track the status of your recycling request by logging into your account and visiting the "My Recycling Requests" section. There, you'll see real-time updates on the status of each request, including when we receive your device, inspection results, and payment processing. You'll also receive email notifications at key stages of the process.

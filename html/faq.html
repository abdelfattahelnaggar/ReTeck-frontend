Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recycling services, payment options, and more.

General Recycling Process Payment & Pricing Shipping & Delivery Account & Security

ReTech is a platform that connects consumers with recycling companies to decrease e-waste and give electronics a new life. We serve as the middleman between you and professional recycling companies. ReTech allows users to upload pictures of their e-waste, receive an estimate of points they can earn, and decide whether to recycle it. If you agree, we arrange for a delivery agent to collect the device and send it to a recycling company for proper handling. To encourage participation, users earn points for their contributions, which they can exchange for vouchers to purchase products from HyperOne market. By making e-waste recycling easy and rewarding, we're helping keep e-waste out of landfills and giving old electronics a second life.

ReTech supports environmental sustainability by connecting individuals and businesses with trusted recycling partners. As a middleman, we ensure electronic waste is handled responsibly by connecting you with recycling companies that specialize in refurbishment, recycling, or safe disposal. By facilitating the reuse of devices and recovery of valuable materials through our recycling partners, we help reduce landfill waste and lower the demand for new resource extraction.

Our network of recycling companies accepts a wide range of electronic devices including smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, kitchen appliances, gaming consoles, televisions, audio equipment, and various small electronics. We connect you with the appropriate recycling partner based on your device type. If you're unsure about a specific device, please contact our customer service for assistance.

Our recycling process is simple: First, submit details about your device through our online form. We'll coordinate with our recycling partners to provide an estimate of points you can earn based on the information provided. Then, ship your device using our prepaid shipping label. Once our recycling partners receive and verify your device's condition, we'll credit the points to your account. Your devices are then sent to our recycling companies for refurbishment or responsible recycling, depending on their condition.

Before sending your device through our service, please follow these steps: 1) Backup your data, 2) Perform a factory reset to remove personal information, 3) Remove any SIM cards or memory cards, 4) Disable any activation locks or Find My Device features, 5) Include the charger and accessories if possible.These steps ensure your device is properly prepared before it reaches our recycling partners.

We take data security very seriously. While we recommend performing a factory reset before sending your device, our recycling company partners also implement a certified data wiping process that meets Department of Defense standards for all devices they receive. We've carefully selected recycling partners that ensure all personal information is completely removed before recycling or refurbishing. As a middleman service, we verify that all recycling companies in our network maintain strict data security protocols.

Instead of monetary payments, we reward you with points for every device you recycle through our platform. The number of points awarded is determined by the make, model, age, condition, and market value of your device. These points accumulate in your account and can be redeemed for vouchers to purchase products from HyperOne market. This reward system encourages sustainable recycling while giving you access to a wide range of products.

You can redeem your points for vouchers in our "My Points" section. Simply log into your account and navigate to the "My Points" section, where you can view your points balance, review your complete points history, and browse available vouchers from HyperOne market. Select a voucher that matches your point balance, and the voucher code will be sent to your email. These vouchers can be used during checkout at HyperOne market, both online and in physical stores, giving you flexibility in how you use your rewards.

If our recycling partners' inspection determines that your device is in worse condition than described, we'll contact you with a revised point offer based on its actual condition. You can choose to accept the new point value or have your device returned to you at no cost. We strive for transparency in our point valuation process to ensure customer satisfaction while maintaining the integrity of our rewards system.

Points earned through recycling are valid for 12 months from the date they are credited to your account. We send reminder emails when your points are approaching expiration to ensure you have the opportunity to redeem them. For loyal customers who regularly recycle through our platform, we occasionally offer point extensions and bonus point promotions, giving you even more value for your recycling efforts.

After accepting our quote, you'll receive a prepaid shipping label via email. Simply package your device securely in a box with proper cushioning, attach the prepaid label, and drop it off at the specified carrier location. For larger items, we can arrange a pickup service. All shipping costs are covered by us. We then coordinate with our recycling partners to ensure your device arrives safely at the appropriate recycling facility.

Yes, all shipments are insured up to the quoted value of your device. In the rare event that a package is lost or damaged during transit, we'll work with the shipping carrier to file a claim and ensure you're compensated appropriately. We recommend taking photos of your device before shipping as additional documentation. This insurance covers the journey from your location to our processing center, where we then distribute devices to our recycling company partners.

The entire process typically takes 7-14 days from the moment you ship your device to when points are credited to your account. This includes shipping time (2-5 days), processing and inspection by our recycling partners (1-2 days), and points crediting (1-2 business days). As the middleman, we coordinate all these steps to make the process as smooth as possible for you. You can track the status of your recycling request through your account dashboard at any time.

Creating an account is easy! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our website. Fill in your details including name, email, and password. Verify your email address through the link we send you, and your account will be ready to use. With an account, you can track multiple recycling requests, manage your payments, and easily connect with our network of recycling companies, all through our platform.

We employ industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Our privacy policy outlines how your data is collected, used, and protected. We never sell your personal information to third parties. All payment processing is done through secure, PCI-compliant systems to ensure the safety of your financial information. We only share necessary information with our recycling partners to facilitate the recycling process, and all our partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Yes, you can track the status of your recycling request by logging into your account and visiting the "My Recycling Requests" section. There, you'll see real-time updates on the status of each request, including when we receive your device, when it's transferred to our recycling partners, inspection results, and payment processing. You'll also receive email notifications at key stages of the process, keeping you informed from the moment you ship your device until it's successfully recycled by our partner companies.
